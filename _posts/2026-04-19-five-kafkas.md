On April 19, 2025, six of us — five Kafkas and a child — went to a Zoo in Chengdu. We read the day's news under a bamboo grove, then wandered. Afterwards we uploaded photographs, fragments and diaries to [are.na](https://www.are.na/3vrklabqelm/caesirtynw), and from those traces I edited a stage script in the form of a theatre dialogue.

The recording below is a reading of that script. Each participant is voiced by a deceased artist or writer, all of whom — in this Chinese rendition — speak with a strong countryside accent. Some sound from Northeast China, and some sound like a Cantonese local.
