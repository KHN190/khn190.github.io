An unofficial performance at an art fair, about four hours, I dressed as [Chibi Maruko-chan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chibi_Maruko-chan), with a poster offering to rent myself for anything at $1 per 10 mins.

A virtual auction followed, where people could tag anybody anything. People hated my - I received 10+ tags — with attacks calling it a commercialism trap, complaints of "cheap value" and "used-ups". The only positive tag came from a child, who liked me for the clown dressing.

I felt unsatisfied with retreated engagement of people. I intended to perform it again on street in the near future.
