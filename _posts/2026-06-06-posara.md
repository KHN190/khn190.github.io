![Posara carts: visuals, sequencer and tracker](/assets/img/machine/posara_showcase.png)

**Posara** is a visualaudio toolkit for creatives — a runtime built on the [Abrase](https://khn190.github.io/abrase/) / Myriad computation core, with early MacOS aesthetics at heart. Carts are written in Abrase, hot-reloaded while you play.

It speaks to both machines and browsers: designed for hardware (bare metal wip) and the Web.

- **Screen** — 1-bit framebuffer
- **Audio** — 4 channels: sample + pitch + stereo volume + ADSR
- **MIDI** — note in / out, talk to your synths
- **Controller** — 8-button bitmap + keys

Other showcase carts include a sequencer cart driving a tracker over MIDI — patterns composed in one screen play and mutate in the other. Written in pure abrase in ~200 lines.

Source is now open at [github.com/KHN190/Posara](https://github.com/KHN190/Posara).

A first album made with Posara is up on SoundCloud: [Posara Vol. 1](https://soundcloud.com/rusty-ocean-blue/sets/posara-vol-1).
