<br>

緋紅的十個太陽，

照亮傾心於黑色的我。

我把劈開的生命，

都獻給了正在舒展的春天。

<br>

緋紅的十個太陽，

在高高的裸岩上旋轉著歌唱。

心中閃過的你，

彷彿紫色的閃電。

---

The ten crimsons,

Shading on me who obsessed with blackness.

I'll devote my whole cleaved life

To the untangling spring.

<br>

The ten crimsons,

Singing high on bare boulders.

Your image fluttered in my heart

Like a purple lightening.
